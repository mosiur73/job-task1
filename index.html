Will it help my snoring? +

While Intake can help improve nasal breathing, which may reduce snoring, it's best to consult with a healthcare professional for severe snoring issues.

Why Nasal Breathing? +

Nasal breathing filters air, warms and humidifies it, and helps produce nitric oxide, which improves oxygen absorption. It's generally healthier than mouth breathing.

Why magnets? +

The magnets in Intake help gently expand the nasal passages, allowing for increased airflow without the need for adhesive strips or internal devices.

How Intake helps +

Intake helps by gently opening your nasal passages, leading to improved airflow, better oxygen intake, reduced mouth breathing, and potentially better sleep and workout performance.

How Long It Take To Work? +

Many users feel a difference immediately upon using Intake. Consistent use over a few days or weeks can lead to more significant and lasting benefits.

How it works +

Intake uses small, comfortable magnets to gently pull open your nasal passages, increasing the space for air to flow through. This can significantly improve your breathing efficiency.
